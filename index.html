Historic Forts ruled by Many Leaders

The first hill, where the main fort is, is called Rajagiri/RajaFort. It is about 800 feet (240 m) in height. The RajaFort is made up of Strong rocks which was built by Soliders. There are also guns and cannonballs made of stone inside the RajaFort. The inner fort consists of Kalyana Mahal, the royal stables, the ruined royal palace, Anaikulam tank, granaries, magazine and the shrine of Venugopalaswami. The Fort is maintained by Archaeological Survey of India(ASI).

The second important hillock with an imposing citadel is known as RaniFort/Krishnagiri.The Krishnagiri fort lies to the North of Tiruvannamalai road. It is Smaller in size When compared with RajaFort/Rajagiri. A flight of steps of granite stones leads to its top. Trekking of RaniFort is Easy when compared with RajaFort.
